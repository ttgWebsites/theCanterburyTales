Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Canterbury Tales
  • Author
  • Geoffrey Chaucer
  • Published
  • The novel was written in the late 1300's, and first published in the early 1400's.
  • Genre
  • Parody, Satire, Romance, as well as Character Portraits.
  • Setting
  • The story is set in the late 1300's at the Tabard Inn as well as the road to Canterbury.
  • Point Of View
  • The story is told from both the first and third person by one of the members of the pilgrims. He or she speaks in the first person in the general prologue and the third when retelling the other group members' tales.
  • Conflict
  • The conflict seen in The Canterbury Tales is mainly between the different members of the group caused by the differences between social classes, moral views, and professions.
